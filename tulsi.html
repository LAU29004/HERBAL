Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Medicinal Uses

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is a revered herb in Ayurvedic medicine. It is known for its numerous health benefits, including:

  • Anti-stress and adaptogenic properties
  • Boosting immune function
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Antioxidant properties
  • Supporting respiratory health

Scientific Name

The scientific name of Tulsi is Ocimum sanctum (also known as Ocimum tenuiflorum).

Habitat

Tulsi is native to the Indian subcontinent and is commonly grown in tropical and subtropical regions. It prefers warm climates and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in home gardens and religious settings in India and other parts of South Asia.
