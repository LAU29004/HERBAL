Lemongrass Information

Medicinal Uses

Lemongrass has various medicinal uses including:

  • Relieving digestive issues such as bloating and indigestion.
  • Reducing anxiety and stress.
  • Acting as an antimicrobial agent.
  • Alleviating symptoms of the common cold and flu.

Scientific Name

The scientific name of lemongrass is Cymbopogon citratus.

Habitat

Lemongrass is native to tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in:

  • Warm climates with plenty of sunlight.
  • Well-drained soil with high organic content.
  • Regions such as Southeast Asia, India, and parts of Africa.
