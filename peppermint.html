Peppermint

Medicinal Uses

Peppermint is a popular herb known for its distinct flavor and numerous health benefits. Its medicinal uses include:

  • Relieving digestive issues such as indigestion and bloating
  • Soothing headaches and migraines
  • Providing relief from respiratory issues like coughs and colds
  • Reducing muscle pain and soreness
  • Acting as an antimicrobial agent

Habitat

Peppermint is native to Europe and the Middle East. It grows best in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun. It is often cultivated in gardens and can thrive in a variety of climates, though it is typically grown in temperate regions.
