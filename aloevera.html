Aloe Vera Information

Medicinal Uses

Aloe Vera is known for its diverse medicinal uses, including:

  • Soothing and healing burns, cuts, and other skin irritations.
  • Hydrating and moisturizing the skin.
  • Possibly aiding in digestion and treating constipation.
  • Providing anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits.

Scientific Name

The scientific name of Aloe Vera is Aloe barbadensis miller.

Habitat

Aloe Vera is native to the Arabian Peninsula but also grows in tropical, semi-tropical, and arid climates. It thrives in:

  • Warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil.
  • Regions such as the Mediterranean, parts of Africa, and the Indian subcontinent.
  • Indoor environments in pots or containers in cooler climates.
